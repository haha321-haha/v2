üåô Menstrual Constitution Adjustment

Characteristics of Different Constitutions During Menstruation

  • Low Energy Pattern: Prolonged periods, heavy flow, pale and thin blood, easy fatigue
  • Cold Sensitivity Pattern: Menstrual cramps, preference for warmth and pressure, cold limbs
  • Overheated Pattern: Early periods, scanty flow, red and thick blood, five-palm heat
  • Circulation Blockage: Severe menstrual cramps, blood clots, dark purple blood
  • Energy Stagnation: Breast tenderness, emotional fluctuations, irritability and crying

Dietary Adjustment Principles for Menstruation

  • Choose foods suitable for your constitution type
  • Avoid raw, cold, spicy, and greasy foods
  • Consume more warm and neutral foods
  • Maintain balanced nutrition and supplement iron

Dietary Recommendations

  • Low Energy Pattern: Eat iron-boosting foods like spinach, dark chocolate, and lean meat
  • Cold Sensitivity Pattern: Eat anti-inflammatory foods like ginger tea and salmon
  • Overheated Pattern: Eat yin-nourishing foods like tremella, lily, and pear
  • Circulation Blockage: Eat blood-activating foods like hawthorn, safflower, and rose
  • Energy Stagnation: Eat qi-regulating foods like tangerine peel, bergamot, and jasmine

Exercise and Fitness Guidance

  • Low Energy Pattern: Choose gentle exercises like Tai Chi and Ba Duan Jin
  • Cold Sensitivity Pattern: Choose aerobic exercises like jogging and swimming
  • Overheated Pattern: Choose static exercises like yoga and meditation
  • Circulation Blockage: Choose blood-activating exercises like dancing and jumping rope
  • Energy Stagnation: Choose joyful exercises like singing and dancing
